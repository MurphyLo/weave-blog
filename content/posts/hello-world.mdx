This is the seed post for the weave blog. It exists to exercise the full
content pipeline: GFM, build-time syntax highlighting, KaTeX math,
footnotes[^1], and the `data-block` selection contract.

## Typography

Body text is set in Inter at 0.875rem with a 460 weight. *Emphasis* swaps to
a serif italic, **strong** stays Inter, and `inline code` uses the mono
stack. Links look like [this one to MDX](https://mdxjs.com).

> Blockquotes mark the inner paragraph as the selectable block, not the
> quote wrapper — blocks never nest.

## Math

Inline math like $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$ flows with the text, while display math
stands alone:

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} \, dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$
